Future Shop
Website

A Canadian electronics store chain with over 130 stores and online eCommerce presence.

The Insight

FutureShop.ca was one of Canada’s largest eCommerce website before its consolidation with Best Buy in March 2015. On a daily basis, it has over 8 million visits a month to research and/or make a purchase online.

Philip was the Senior User Interface Designer on FutureShop.ca. His focus was to set standards for FutureShop.ca’s visual design team, establish user flow for daily promotional campaigns and monitor monthly statistics on our traffic and engagement to provide business team some traffic insight.

The Challenge

Data is Always Important

As a Senior User Interface Designer, Philip has to monitor FutureShop.ca’s data on a monthly basis to to understand visitations and break down the traffics based on browsers, OS and devices to provide full list of browsers and Devices support for our QA team.

In addition, Philip reviews periodically on the site's activity maps to understand user’s engagement in the site to provide recommendations on visual treatments and ordering of elements to enhance the user experience.

Visits percentage for the Month of May 2014.

Web Style Guide Establishment

As one of Canada’s largest eCommerce website with over 12 designers to keep up with business command, many of them are junior/intermediate level talents. There was a need to establish a web style guide that sets and enforces all FutureShop.ca visual and coding standard to support daily promotional campaigns in a consistent and efficient manner.

Philip’s Web Style Guide is the foundation to everything on FutureShop.ca, from design principle, typography, font size, colour, grid system and many more. Designers fully utilize all elements specified in the guide to mix and match different functional templates together to come up with visual treatments that serves different business’s needs.

The Result

While FutureShop.ca era come to an end, Philip’s contribution to the team and dedications to set, monitor and enforce visual standards within the team didn’t get unnoticed as commented by his peers.
