Yishu: Journal of Chinese Contemporary Art

The first English language journal to focus on contemporary Chinese art and culture.

The Insight

Yishu is a respected journal devoted to Chinese contemporary Art. Since the inauguration in May 2002, it has attracted not only professionals in the art and scholars but also art enthusiasts in general. Yishu continues to bring artists together to express ideas and communicate them through arts & cultures.

Philip has been the lead designer for every Yishu Journal since 2007, participating on content structure, layout design and advertisement design.

The Idea

Yishu is one of a kind journal in the market focusing on Chinese contempoary arts written in English language. To capture this distinct position, the journal is designed in a unique retangular 7" by 12" dimension that is uncommon to other collections in the industry.

Different layout design samples.

Systematic Colour Grouping

Since 2002, over 72 journals were published. For scholars and art professions, it is important to research and reference old artifacts and articles. With 72 journals stacked on the shelve it can be difficult to reference quickly. As a result, a systematic colour grouping is designed to differentiate each year's Yishu Journals. This allows visual connection for readers to easily remember which color scheme the articles they read belongs to; hence, narrowing the search from all journals to just 6.

Thanks for your work and patience on this issue; I am glad it is done!

Keith Wallace, Editor-in-Chief

The Result

This unique 7" by 12" design Journal has bring a very distinct look to Yishu Journal, which separates themselves from the rest of the competitors while still providing a solid framework to built upon for every journal. Regardless of what the content might be on a bi-monthly basis, the layout is able to support them with ease. This brings a very consistent and systematic structure that is crucial to a valuable and resourceful collection for readers to understand easily.

The colour scheme offered a great visual aid for readers to reference reader's desired journal quickly. In addition, it was aesthetically pleasing to bring a deeper level of grouping as we view the entire Yishu Journals Collection together.
