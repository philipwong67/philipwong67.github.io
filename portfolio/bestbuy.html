Best Buy Canada
Website

Best Buy is one of Canada’s largest and most successful retailers, operating the Best Buy, Best Buy Mobile, and Geek Squad brands.

The Insight

Best Buy Canada is an award winning omni channel retail company in Canada with over 10 million visits a month.

Philip is currently the Digital Visual Lead for BestBuy.ca who leads a team of senior designers to set standards for all visual presentation on BestBuy.ca, establish and review user flow for major promotional events such as Black Friday/Boxing Day Sales and monitor the site’s analytics to incorporate changes that positively impacts the BestBuy.ca.

The Challenges

Black Friday Sales

Black Friday Sales has become a big trend in Canada. Best Buy Canada has been the pioneer for introducing in Canada in BestBuy.ca. Philip’s focus is establish a recommended user flow for business team to establish a merchandising strategy around it.

The homepage of BestBuy.ca during Black Friday Sale requires special consideration in order to meet these business objectives:

  1. Funnel customers to the categories they are looking for,
  2. Showcase the hottest doorcrashers and link directly to the product detail page.

Make improvements with Data

As the Digital Visual Design Lead, Philip monitors data on a monthly basis or by special request on a particular aspect of the site to gather insights when it comes to UX/UI decisions. He reviews the site’s activity maps to understand user’s engagement in BestBuy.ca to provide recommendations on visual treatments that better suits user’s need and meeting business goals.

The results of reviewing the current Shop by Brands Dropdown Menu’s clicks and recommendations made to meet business’s goal of highlighting and improving clicks by 20%. Before (First) and after (Last)

Web Style Guide Establishment

The Web Style Guide was an initiative Philip established as he carry over from the Future Shop Brand to set, monitor and enforce UI Standard and supports daily promotional campaigns. It consists of design principle, typography, font size, colour, grid system and many more.

Not only does our designers fully utilize this on a daily basis, the marketing team and third party developers also utilize this guide to understand and develop Best Buy related projects to provide a consistent digital experience.
