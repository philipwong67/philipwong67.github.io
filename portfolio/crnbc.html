College of Registered Nurses in British Columbia

Protect the public through the regulation of registered nurses, nurse practitioners and licensed graduate nurses.

The Insight

CRNBC approached has a new initiative to establish eLearning materials on the web platform for all registered nurses and nurse practitioners within British Columbia to review their daily nursing practice.

The Idea

Maintenance, scalability, those are the criteria that must be accounted for. A modular approach took place so that all topics and contents inside them are organized as individual components, this allows flexibility to mix and match different pieces together to compile one eLearning Module for each topic.

Omni Channel Approach

In addition to the eLearning modules, each of them consists of two PDF documents available for print. This execution welcomes nurses and nurses practitioners to have positive and open discussions with colleagues and peers while also reviewing the eLearning Module on their own online.

The Result

With over 36,000 nurses and nurse practitioners, it is important to gather feedback to determine the effectiveness of these eLearning modules. An individual survey system is implemented to gather valuable feedback for further improvements on the content and user experience of these modules.
