British Columbia Health Regulators

Representing 23 regulatory colleges governed by the Health Professions Act and the Social Workers Act.

The Insight

A group of BC Health Professions now unites together as one identity to provide health care services to the public of BC. As part of the Leap Creative Group team, Philip was involved to establish a unified brand that will represent them all.

The Idea

They make sure licensed professionals are qualified to follow their standards. Print promotional samples.

The Web Platform

The primarily UX focus of the website is to provide relevant information to both desktop users and mobile users. Specifically for seniors in need of health care information online, having legible text on all device and ease of navigation is key. In addition, all information needs to support multi-language for those who are has difficulties understanding English.

The Result

A one destination stop that provides all the necessary information regarding to BC health issues and an easy site navigation to find more information to other BC health regulatory. The mobile web layout provide a much needed experience for elders who are looking for information on their mobile devices. The multi-language support in PDF format turns out to be a smart decision with informations easily accessible for the smaller target group while not over investing for a multi-language platform.
